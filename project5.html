Infinite Reach

Project Screenshot

Infinite Reach is an accessibility-focused mobile application designed to assist individuals with visual, hearing, or mobility impairments. It provides voice navigation, gesture-based controls, and emergency support to help users interact with their surroundings independently and safely. The app aims to bridge accessibility gaps through inclusive design and smart assistive tools.

Key Features:

• Voice Navigation & Audio Feedback: For visually impaired users to navigate their environment safely.

• Sign-to-Text Support: To aid hearing-impaired communication in real-time.

• Gesture-Based Controls: For users with mobility challenges to interact with the app easily.

• Emergency SOS Alerts: With location sharing for immediate assistance.

• Accessible UI: With customizable contrast and font options for better visibility.

Tech Stack:
Flutter, Firebase, Google Accessibility APIs, TensorFlow (for gesture/sign recognition), Python
