Smart Composting Dustbin

3D Model Demo

Smart Composting Dustbin is an IoT-based solution that processes biodegradable waste into ash using controlled heating. It helps track the quantity of waste processed and calculates revenue based on the ash produced. Designed for household or community use, it promotes eco-friendly waste management with economic benefits.

Key Features:

• Converts organic waste into ash: Using thermal processing.

• Real-time tracking: Of waste volume and revenue.

• Energy-efficient heating mechanism: For optimal performance.

• LED/LCD indicators: For bin status monitoring.

• Ideal for community use: Streets, schools, or homes.

Tech Stack:
Arduino/Raspberry Pi, Sensors (Temperature, Humidity), Embedded C, Firebase (for data logging), LCD/LED modules
